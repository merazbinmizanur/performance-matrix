Rules of Engagement

Official Protocol Guidelines

  • Match Limits

    You can play a maximum of 5 Matches against the exact same opponent per ranking cycle.

  • Scoring Protocol

    Victory = 3 PTS  •  Draw = 1 PT  •  Defeat = 0 PTS

  • Ranking Algorithm

    Leaderboard standings prioritize: Points → Goal Diff (GD) → Goals Scored → Wins.

  • Matrix Cycles

    Competitive leaderboards and stats automatically reset at the end of every Week and Month.

  • Result Verification

    Your Secret Matrix ID is strictly required to submit and verify final match scores.

  • Match Settings

    Time: 8 Mins  •  Condition: Normal  •  ET & Penalty: OFF

  • Weekly Star Bonus

    The Weekly Winner receives 1 Star at the end of the week, adding +10 PTS directly to their Monthly Matrix.
